About Crom's Overtures

Crom's Overtures is dedicated to creating epic fantasy artwork inspired by the (uncensored) illustrators of the 1970s and 1980s, Boris Vallejo, Frank Frazetta, Vicente Segrelles, the spirit of Sword and Sorcery, the style of Buscema, or more recently Gerardo Zaffino, and all the lit literature of Tolkien, Leiber, Ashton Smith, Eddison, Vance, and of course Howard, I add a good dose of Caravaggio's chiaroscuro to achieve a kind of personal style: Sword and Tenebrism.

Our passion for mythic tales and powerful visuals drives us to craft illustrations that capture the raw essence of adventure and heroism. Stay tuned for more about our journey and creative process.
